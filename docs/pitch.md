# Local talent,<br/>*actually* local.

The verified marketplace for Vancouver's creator economy.

*Seed round · 2026*

---

## The problem

- **Customers** can't find trustworthy local help. Star ratings ≠ trust.
- **Creators** get squeezed: TaskRabbit takes 15%, Thumbtack lead fees nickel-and-dime, Care.com has a $39/month paywall just to message.
- **Existing platforms** are designed for global scale, not local trust.

> *"I just want a cleaner my neighbour vouches for."* — every customer, ever.

---

## Our solution

**Gigsta** is a Vancouver-first marketplace where every creator is **verified** —
government ID, professional credentials, insurance where required.

- **For customers**: discover, book, pay, all in one place. Verified profiles only.
- **For creators**: keep **92%** of every booking. Standard liability insurance included.
- **For the city**: a directory of trusted local talent.

---

## How it works

1. Customer browses by category &amp; neighborhood
2. Books a service — pays online or in person
3. Creator delivers; both leave feedback
4. Gigsta takes a flat **8% on Pro** (vs 15-30% elsewhere)

No lead fees. No premium paywalls. No surprise charges.

---

## Why now

- **$455B gig economy** globally; **22% YoY growth** in Canada (Statistics Canada, 2025)
- **AI-assisted onboarding** makes creator verification 10x cheaper than it was 5 years ago
- **Local-first SEO** wins post-Google algorithm changes (favouring brand-direct traffic)
- **Vancouver has a $4.2B/yr informal services economy** — most of it cash, untracked, friction-heavy

## Business model

Three revenue streams:

1. **Take rate** — 8% on Pro plan transactions (vs 15-30% incumbents)
2. **Creator subscriptions** — $10/mo Pro, $30/mo VIP (featured)
3. **Premium services** — verified background checks, sponsored category placement, B2B (corporate bookings)

Gross margin > 80% after Stripe and verification costs.
