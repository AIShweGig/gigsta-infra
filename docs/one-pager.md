## What it is

Gigsta is a verified marketplace connecting Vancouver residents with local creators
across nine categories: culinary, home services, cleaning, dog walking, childcare,
eldercare, tutoring, gardening, and events.

Every creator on Gigsta has been verified — government ID, professional credentials,
and insurance where required. Customers can browse, book, and pay in one place.

## Why it matters

Incumbents (TaskRabbit, Thumbtack, Care.com) optimize for global scale at the cost of
local trust. They take 15–30% per transaction or charge customers monthly paywalls.
Gigsta flips this: **creators keep 92%**, customers pay nothing to browse and book,
and trust comes from real verification — not just a star rating.
